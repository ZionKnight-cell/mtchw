Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

mtchw is a simple boredom-button app that gives you one small activity to do when you are bored. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is stored.

1. Information mtchw collects

The current version of mtchw does not require an account, login, email address, phone number, name, profile, or cloud sync.

mtchw does not collect personal information through the app in this MVP version.

2. Information stored on your device

mtchw stores a small amount of app data locally on your device so the app can remember your experience.

This local app data may include:

  • Saved activities
  • Activity history
  • Your last selected activity preferences

This information stays on your device. It is not sent to a mtchw server because the MVP does not use a backend server or account system.

3. How the data is used

Local app data is used only to:

  • Show your saved activities
  • Show your activity history
  • Remember your last selected energy, time, and category choices
  • Help avoid showing the same activities too often

4. Clearing your data

You can clear saved activities from the Saved tab. You can clear activity history from the History tab.

You can also uninstall the app to remove the app and its local data from your device.

5. Data sharing

mtchw does not sell your data. mtchw does not share your saved activities, history, or preferences with advertisers or social media platforms.

6. Analytics and ads

The current MVP version of mtchw does not include advertising, third-party analytics, user tracking, subscriptions, payments, chat, social feeds, or user accounts.

7. Website hosting note

The public web version of mtchw may be served through a hosting provider. Hosting providers may process basic technical information needed to serve the website, such as request logs. mtchw does not use this information to create personal profiles.

8. Children

mtchw is a general-audience app. It is not designed to collect information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if mtchw adds new features such as accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or optional online services.

10. Contact

Before publishing on Google Play, replace this line with your real support email address.

Developer note: this is a practical starter privacy policy for the current MVP. Review it before publishing and update it if the app adds analytics, ads, accounts, cloud sync, AI features, or any third-party SDKs.
